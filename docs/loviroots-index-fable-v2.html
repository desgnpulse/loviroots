Chapter I ❧ The shelf

What goes in

Five things, known well.

Every Lovi recipe is built from the same short shelf. Different products take different measures — a little more of this, a steep of that — but the shelf never grows longer than it needs to be.

  • No. 01

    Shea butter

    Butyrospermum parkii

    The base

    Unrefined and nutty, dense with the fats skin recognises. Every recipe in the book starts here.

  • No. 02

    Aloe vera

    Aloe barbadensis

    The calm

    The leaf generations reach for first — cooling, settling, kind to skin that has had a long day.

  • No. 03

    Coconut oil

    Cocos nucifera

    The softener

    Melts on contact and carries the blend, so the finish lands light instead of waxy.

  • No. 04

    Herbs

    grown & gathered

    The tradition

    Steeped in, never sprinkled on. The quiet detail that makes one recipe different from the next.

  • No. 05

    Essential oils

    pressed & distilled

    The signature

    A few drops, chosen per recipe. How you'd know a Lovi jar with your eyes closed.

Nothing on the shelf hides behind a code name — and the full list for each recipe lives on its jar.

Chapter III ❧ The hands

The Loviroots story

Before it was a label, it was a habit.

The recipes in this book were never developed. They were remembered — measured in palms and pinches long before grams, corrected by feel, passed along whenever someone left home with dry hands.

We named the company Loviroots because that is what these recipes are: roots. Writing them down — and whipping them in small batches — is just our way of letting them travel further than one kitchen, without changing what they are.
